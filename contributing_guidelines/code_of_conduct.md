# Code of Conduct

We are committed to making this a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for all contributors regardless of background or experience level.

**We expect all contributors to:**

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

**We will not tolerate:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to the community's purpose
- Deliberately misleading or low-quality content submissions

Violations may result in your contribution being rejected or your access to the project being revoked. If you witness or experience a violation, please open a private issue or contact a maintainer directly.
