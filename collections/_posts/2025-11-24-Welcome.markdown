Welcome to the first blog post for the UK Data Linkage Community.

The community is built on years of prior discussions, collaboration, and networking within the data linkage space.

The community is funded by DARE UK as part of the ongoing work to support community groups in fostering collaboration, 
capacity building and public trust in the UK's sensitive data research. Check out the 
[announcement blog from DARE UK][dare-announcement] for more information.

### <u>Getting Involved</u>
To sign up to the UK DLC mailing list, [please use the following form][ukdlc-mailing-list].

The community can be found on GitHub as [UK-Data-Linkage-Community][ukdlc-github]
