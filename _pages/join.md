To register your interest with the UK Data Linkage Community, please [sign up for the mailing list](https://forms.gle/KjNE5eEL3nUBqGat8).

All resources produced as part of the UK DLC project, including this website, are hosted as part of a GitHub organisation at [https://github.com/UK-Data-Linkage-Community](https://github.com/UK-Data-Linkage-Community).

<hr style="border-top: 1px solid {{ site.ukdlc_color_grey }};"> 

## Engaging with the community
To get involved with community activities, we welcome contact [via email](mailto:{{ site.contact_email }}?subject=UK DLC) or through the [GitHub discussions]({{ site.ukdlc_github_discussions }}).

Please consider engaging with the community to:
* suggest materials or resources you'd like to see
* request insights into linkage approaches from community members
* share experiences or challenges within your own in linkage pipelines
* identify areas for the community to prioritise
